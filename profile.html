Hello, My name is Lesley

Welcome to my portfolio. I’m a passionate software engineer based in Los Angeles, CA. With a unique journey from psychology to the forefront of technology, I am thrilled at the prospect of joining the tech field as an iOS developer. My passion lies in leveraging my nontraditional background and newfound technical skills to create inclusive, user-centric solutions.

Diverse Pathway Into Tech

My pivot to technology began with a transformative 14-week course with Code First Girls, an organization dedicated to closing the gender gap in tech through free education. There, I embraced the mission to bridge the gender gap in technology, gaining not just technical skills but also a deep commitment to inclusivity and empowerment in the tech industry. This journey has equipped me with a versatile array of skills (including but not limited to):

  • Python
  • SQL
  • JavaScript
  • Swift

Currently I’ve enrolled into DevMountain, a bootcamp to solidify the skills I’ve gained and foster new technical expertise, such a gaining a specialization in iOS development.
